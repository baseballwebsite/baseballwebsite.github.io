Pro Rules:

New rules are now being placed for the 2020 season such as the three-batter minimum that now states that a pitcher has to face three batters before they can be removed. There are also new roster rules such as the 14-pitcher limit in 28 person rosters and 13 in 25. Pitchers are also now having a 15 day minimum rest when they are injured.


For more info visit: https://www.mlb.com/news/mlb-rule-changes-for-2020-season
