Odd Rules:

There are many weird rules in all levels of baseball or unspoken rules. Some examples are if the ball becomes dirty it must be replaced. On average the ball is replaces 70 times. Also, if the catcher gets hit by the bat it is seen as their fault. The pitchers have the weirdest rules about politeness and procedures. Another is the umpires are not allowed to speak to others during the game.
